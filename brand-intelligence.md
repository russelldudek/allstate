## Official logo source and local path
- Source: official Allstate agent-domain asset above.
- Local path: `assets/brand/allstate-hands.png`.
- Use: visible above-fold employer identification and restrained document identifier.
- Treatment: byte-for-byte downloaded PNG; proportions and colors preserved; no tracing, recoloring, distortion, crop, animation, or combination into a candidate mark.

## Visible company identity
The official Allstate hands mark appears with the typed company/role context and the explicit qualifier `Candidate vision ... by Russell Dudek`. The campaign is labeled as independent and not an Allstate publication or endorsement.

## Color-token provenance
- `#00339F` - source-sampled dominant official blue from the official Allstate PNG.
- `#001F5B` - source-sampled dark-blue recognition value from the official mark's dark pixels and used as a supporting brand tone.
- White and light blue-gray neutrals - document/interface neutrals.
- `#F2B544`, `#2AA895`, `#D45C5C`, and `#07162E` - candidate-created trace, state, and background colors tied to the AI Action Flight Recorder. They remain subordinate to Allstate blue.

## Typography evidence and decision
Allstate's current digital presence uses a friendly, humanist sans-serif character with rounded forms and strong sentence-case hierarchy. The proprietary production font is not copied or committed. The campaign uses the installed open Lato family, then Noto Sans and broad system fallbacks, as a structural substitute. No font binaries are included.

## Photography and illustration character
The campaign does not use stock photography. Recognition comes from the official mark, blue/white identity, generous consumer-facing spacing, humanist typography, and clear rounded geometry. The candidate-original AI Action Flight Recorder provides the role-specific technical visual system.

## Geometry and interface motifs
- rounded containers and pill controls derived from Allstate's approachable digital character;
- strong blue fields with white space;
- transparent layered technical surfaces for platform planes;
- perforated action record and control plates for traceability;
- restrained line work and high contrast.

## Motion character
Purposeful, calm, and legible. The recorder checks identity/context/authority/assurance and emits a trace. Scenario selection changes authority, controls, release posture, and required record. Reduced-motion mode resolves all states without animation.

## Independent-candidate distinction
The campaign uses Allstate identity only for nominative identification of the target employer. It does not imitate official navigation, legal furniture, portals, or letterhead and does not imply endorsement.

## Recommended use by artifact
- Website: full Allstate blue recognition layer plus official mark and recorder metaphor.
- Resume / cover letter: restrained official mark, blue rules, Lato hierarchy, and candidate-owned header.
- Interview brief / entry plan / review artifact: same restrained mark, rules, tables, and trace vocabulary.
