Final review questions

  • Is the authority level proportional to consequence, reversibility, evidence, and recovery?
  • Does the trace support a real decision, or merely create observability volume?
  • Is the paved path easier than a local workaround?
  • Does the cost unit reflect completed work, not only token use?
  • What should the platform learn from this workload?
