Allstate Hiring Team
Principal Platform Consultant, AI Platforms

Allstate's challenge is no longer whether generative AI can be useful. Public reporting shows AI already participating in high-volume claimant communication with human verification, while the role itself calls for enterprise reference architectures, model access and orchestration, agent workflows, identity, auditability, observability, resilience, and cost efficiency. The harder question is how to let those capabilities move faster without making accountability disappear.

My point of view is straightforward: an enterprise AI platform should be built around an inspectable action contract. Every consequential AI interaction should make the actor, context, model route, tools, authority boundary, evaluation evidence, fallback, owner, outcome, and unit economics replayable. That creates a paved road rather than a governance tollbooth - and it gives product, engineering, security, risk, and business leaders a shared basis for deciding what may scale.

My verified experience approaches that problem from several relevant directions. Through DudeWorth, I directly design AI-readiness, RAG, agentic workflow, human-judgment, escalation, evaluation, decision-record, and governance patterns. At Vape-Jet, I lead AI-first operating transformation across production, quality, customer success, engineering issue flow, ERP, collaboration systems, telemetry, and a software-enabled robotics fleet. At Amazon, I built operating mechanisms in a 24/7 customer-backward environment supporting approximately five million annual second-day orders. At Compunetics, I led engineering, manufacturing, quality, and high-reliability technical programs where controls, traceability, and failure discipline were not optional.

I would not represent that record as direct recent ownership of an Allstate-scale multi-cloud LLM gateway, vector platform, or production agent infrastructure. That is the reasonable objection. The case for interviewing me is that I know how to work directly in emerging-technology problems, translate across executive and technical stakeholders, bind innovation to governed work, and install mechanisms that make scale visible and manageable. A bounded architecture working session would be the right test.

My independent candidate vision at https://russelldudek.github.io/allstate/ makes that reasoning inspectable through an AI Action Flight Recorder, an authority-sensitive scenario model, a 120-day entry plan, and an AI Action Trace Review. The work is a transparent hypothesis for discovery, not a claim about Allstate's undisclosed architecture.

I would welcome the opportunity to discuss how Allstate can make the safest, most observable, and most reusable AI path the fastest path for its teams.

Carpe diem,
